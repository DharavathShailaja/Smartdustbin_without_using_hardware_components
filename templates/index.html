Welcome to AI Smart Dustbin

The AI Smart Dustbin is an innovative system that uses sensors, AI, and QR code technology to detect waste levels, identify locations, and monitor bins in real-time through an interactive dashboard. This project can also be developed and simulated without hardware components, making it ideal for showcasing smart waste management using software-based automation and AI concepts.

About AI Smart Dustbin

The AI Smart Dustbin is designed to make waste management smarter and more efficient. It uses sensors to detect when the dustbin is full, cameras to capture waste particles, and QR codes for easy identification and location tracking. Our goal is to reduce human effort and promote cleanliness through automation.
